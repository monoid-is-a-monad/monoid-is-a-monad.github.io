### Who we are
For over three decades, Karathanasis Driving School has helped Toumpa residents drive with confidence. Based at Anatolikis Thrakis 49, we know the local streets, the exam routes, and how to build your confidence at any age.

### Our approach
- Human, step-by-step coaching.
- Balanced theory and practice so you feel ready for real conditions.
- Constantly updated material with mock tests that mirror the exam.

### The instructors
- Experienced, patient, and focused on practical examples.
- Certified for car (B) and motorcycle (A) training on manual vehicles.
- Committed to safety and solid habits from your first drive.

### Our promise
To guide you with clear steps, real care, and actionable feedback so you pass your exams and feel safe every day on the road.
